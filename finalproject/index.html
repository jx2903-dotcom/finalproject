City dogs. Gentle grooming.

Urban Paws Grooming is a small dog grooming studio in New York City focused on calm, low-stress care for city dogs. We keep appointments short, use simple products, and always move at each dog's pace.

This demo website introduces our philosophy, shows sample grooming packages, and lets visitors book an appointment or send questions online.
